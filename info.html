who is spencer ~

spencer balderrama is a common human creating prints, objects and media projects with an emphasis on modern arts and technology.

#BornInMexico and raised in the north he does design stuff, specialising on branding , UI, UX, and cartel visual communication.

The goal is making advanced, researched and sublime design hand by hand of technology and globalization world following its changes. Following the philosophy "think more, design less".

Also taking hot photos as @3dkenzo and playing in clubs as DJ SPENCER.

"i'm a half designer, half engineer and half pig."
